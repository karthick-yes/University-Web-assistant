Syllabus: Review of vectors and matrices. Curves and surfaces. Partial derivatives. Total differential and gradients. Maximum and minimum values. Lagrange multipliers. Double integrals, Fubini’s theorem. Line integrals in the plane. Green’s theorem. Triple integrals and surface integrals in 3-space. Stokes theorem. Applications of multivariable calculus.
